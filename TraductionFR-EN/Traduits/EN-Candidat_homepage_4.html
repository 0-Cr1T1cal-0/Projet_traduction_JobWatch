WHO IS JOB WATCH?

Jobwatch.ch is a place where candidates, companies and trainers meet, with the goal of providing privileged access to employment and personalised services to our more than 41,000 candidates.

Our vast network of partner companies - over 450 to date - allow us to offer a wide range of professional opportunities across all areas of watchmaking and microtechnology. Whether in technical trades, marketing and sales or in R&D, you'll find the perfect platform on jobwatch.ch.

We also offer a growing number of training courses through several partners, renowned in their fields: spanning technology, management and personal development. This allows our candidates to keep their knowledge up-to-date and stand out from the crowd.

That's why Jobwatch.ch has been the leading recruitment site in the world of watchmaking and microtechnology for more than 12 years.
